About 

The first thoughts for RePsychle were conceptualized in 2018 while working in a restaurant as a Line Cook.

When working in an environment that is constantly moving, it became clear that a lot of products that were being used did not have the environment in mind. The majority of the products were either not being recycled properly or weren't able to recycle properly due to inconsistent messaging based on perception or the region I was located in.

I knew that there must be a better way to organize the ways to recycle objects based on a general and specific-use basis.

RePsychle was created to develop and implement the tools for users to use to make better choices when it comes to recycling.
