How to RePsychle

To have the best experience with the RePsychle platform, it is recommended to follow these steps. Please be aware that while you can view the RePsychle Database as a guest, you won't be able to create any entries.

Start using RePsychle in 5 Steps 


Account Creation

While you are able to view the RePsychle Database without needing to create an account, it's important for the stability of the platform for diverse individuals to have the opportunity to create entries.

Creating an Account is Simple:

1) Navigate to the Registration Page located in the Header, Footer, or click here.

2) Provide a valid email address and a secure password. Please avoid re-using a password that is used on another site.

3) Wait for the Confirmation Page to notify you to check your Inbox for an Activation Link

4) Once complete, you can login using your new credentials!

Viewing Database Entries

The RePsychle Database displays entries based on the following parameters:

-  Name of Object
- Date of Upload
- EcoScore
- Location
- Disposal Method
- User of Upload

When filtering or searching for entries, you will be able to click on an object to navigate to a specific page that loads that entry. By doing so, you can view all the details of the entry.

Creating a New Database Entry

In order to create a new RePsychle Database entry, you will need to ensure that you are logged into your account.

If you do not have an account with us, please create one by navigating the header/footer for the Sign-Up portal or by clicking here.

In order to create an entry, please navigate to the RePsychle Database page located in the Footer, or click here.

You will require the following information:

- Name of the Object
- Material of the Object
- Disposal Method of the Object
- EcoScore of the Object
- Regional Location of the Object
- An image for identification purposes

Once your entry has been submitted, then it will be available for other RePsychlers to use.

Managing Database Entries

When viewing an entry that is within the RePsychle Database, you may see things differently if you are the original creator of the entry and are currently logged in.

If you are the original creator of the entry, you will be given the option to select your entry to either do the following:

- Update information for the Entry
- Delete the Entry

My Account is a portal that you can use to view your contributions to the RePsychle Database.

Please be aware that if you do decide to delete your account, the entries that are tied to your account will also be deleted.

Manage Your Account

The My Account portal is more than just a place to view your contributions to the RePsychle Database. You can also view your account and make any changes as needed. You will have the ability to perform the following:

- Change your Account Picture
- Change your Email Address
- Change your Password

You will also have the ability to delete your account within this portal. Please note that by deleting your account through this portal, you will also be deleting any entries that may be associated to your account!
