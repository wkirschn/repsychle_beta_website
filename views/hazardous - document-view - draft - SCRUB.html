Hazardous Objects

The unfortunate reality of the world that we currently reside in is that we generate chemicals and compounds that are not only harmful to the earth, but they can also endanger your health and the health of others!

While similar products may have a pattern of other compounds and chemicals, the usage of these products can be harmful in a variety of ways.

A great example of this is quick cement. While the contents are similar, various providers add their own formulas and mixtures that can be deadly to breathe in.

Make sure to always follow the instructions and to adhere to the warnings to call your Local Poison Hotline if you perform an action that can harm the health of yourself or of others.

During this Beta, we recommend following these guidelines:


      Double-check with your region before making an entry to ensure that an object cannot be disposed of in another way.

     If it can be separated into components, then sort them as needed.

EcoScore Rating

An object that has been rated VERY HIGH has an extremely positive impact on the environment. This object is assumed to have been created with minimal impact on the atmosphere, is composed of only one or a few materials, and can be passed on to the next phase of its life cycle in a positive manner if disposed of using the provided disposal method.

An object that has been rated HIGH has a positive impact on the environment. This object is assumed to have been created with a low impact on the atmosphere, is composed of a few materials, and can be passed on to the next phase of its life cycle in a specific way if disposed of properly.

An object that has been rated MEDIUM has a moderate impact on the environment. This object is assumed to have been created with a measurable impact on the atmosphere, is composed of more than a few materials, and can be passed on to the next phase of its life cycle if cleaned, prepared, and disposed of in a specific manner.

An object that has been rated LOW has a heavy impact on the environment. This object is assumed to have been created to be reactive with the atmosphere, is composed of various compounds, and can be passed on to the next phase of its life cycle if cleaned, handled, prepared, and disposed of in a specific manner. Even if this object is disposed of properly, this object should be avoided unless necessary.

An object that has been rated VERY LOW has an extreme impact on the environment. This object is assumed to have been created to be reactive or corrosive to the atmosphere, is composed of various compounds or formulas, and can be passed on to the next phase of its life cycle if cleaned, handled, prepared, and disposed of in a very specific manner. Even if this object is disposed of properly, this object should be avoided unless necessary. Follow all warnings, directions, and disposal methods.
